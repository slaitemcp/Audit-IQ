The problem
Every auditor knows this pain
Days of post-audit admin
Audit ends Friday. Report lands Tuesday. Findings typed from scribbled notes. Findings emailed from memory — missing half the evidence.
Four separate checklists
ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 all share the same High-Level Structure. Auditors work through Clause 7 three times. Hours of duplicated effort, every single audit.
No live scoring
Compliance percentages calculated by hand after the fact. No way to show the client a live score during the closing meeting. No trend data for surveillance audits.
Findings buried in Word
Major findings mixed with observations in a 40-page document. No central register. No filter by severity. No way to know which findings are still open six months later.
The solution
Everything an auditor needs.
Nothing they don't.
ISO 19011 Audit Plan — generated in 60 seconds
Fill in client name, date and scope. AuditIQ generates a compliant audit plan — schedule, clauses, objectives, signature blocks — fully editable and ready to email to the auditee before the audit.
Integrated audit — all 4 standards, one checklist
HLS clauses (§4–10) mapped across ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 simultaneously. ISO 3834-2 all 18 clauses sit alongside. Audit shared clauses once. Four certificates. One workflow.
Quick view & guided view — adapts to your experience
Experienced auditor? Switch to Quick view — clause ref and key evidence only, fast and clean. Less experienced? Guided view shows full grouped evidence sub-questions per clause. One toggle, same scoring.
Photo evidence — instant on tablet or phone
Tap the camera icon on any clause. On a tablet the camera opens directly — photograph the nonconforming weld, the missing record, the damaged equipment. Photo attaches to that clause instantly. Compressed and stored.
Audit history — year-on-year per client
Every completed audit is saved to the client's history. Year-on-year compliance trend charts, per-standard score tables, findings trend and automatic repeat finding detection — ready before the opening meeting.
Consultative gap report — guided actions per clause
Not just scores. Each gap gets a plain-language recommended action, risk level, responsible party and timeline. A document worth R8,000–R15,000 that AuditIQ generates automatically at the close of audit.
Executive report — written for MD and board level
Overall verdict in plain English, traffic light per standard, top 3 business risks, 90-day action roadmap and sign-off block. Ready at the closing meeting. Not 3 weeks later.
Supplier audit — Mode A & Mode B
Mode A for certified suppliers — ISO 9001 process audit only, no duplication. Mode B for uncertified — full evaluation across all applicable clauses. Supplier audit plan emailed before the visit.
Email everything — before you leave the site
Email the executive report, findings register, gap report and audit plan — all from inside AuditIQ. Pre-populated with all audit data. No Word documents. No 3-week wait. Done at the closing meeting.
