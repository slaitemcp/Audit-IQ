The problem
Every auditor knows this pain
Days of post-audit admin
Audit ends Friday. Report lands Tuesday. Findings typed from scribbled notes. Findings emailed from memory — missing half the evidence.
Four separate checklists
ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 all share the same High-Level Structure. Auditors work through Clause 7 three times. Hours of duplicated effort, every single audit.
No live scoring
Compliance percentages calculated by hand after the fact. No way to show the client a live score during the closing meeting. No trend data for surveillance audits.
Findings buried in Word
Major findings mixed with observations in a 40-page document. No central register. No filter by severity. No way to know which findings are still open six months later.
The solution
Everything an auditor needs.
Nothing they don't.
ISO 19011 Audit Plan — generated in 60 seconds
Fill in client name, date, scope. AuditIQ generates a compliant audit plan — schedule, clauses, scope, objectives, signature blocks — ready to send to the auditee. No template hunting.
Integrated audit — all 4 standards, one checklist
HLS clauses (4–10) are mapped across ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 simultaneously. Audit shared clauses once. ISO 3834-2 welding clauses sit alongside. One workflow. Four certificates.
Auto-findings — the moment you score
Score a clause 0 and a Major finding is created instantly. Score 1 — Minor. Score 2 — OFI. Auditor edits the note. No manual classification. No missed findings. The register builds itself.
Live compliance scores — per clause, per standard
Every clause scored 0–3 converts to a percentage in real time. Score cards update as you work. Walk into the closing meeting with the full report already on screen.
Email findings on-site — before you leave
One click generates a pre-populated finding email or full audit report email. Send individual findings or all findings at once. Mailto or copy-to-clipboard. Done at the closing meeting.
Supplier audit — dedicated template
Same four standards, separate supplier-specific report output. Evaluate your supply chain against ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, and 3834-2 welding requirements — with a report your procurement team can use.
Audit history — trend data for surveillance
Every completed audit saved and searchable. Show your certification body a compliance trend line across five audits. Management review input generated automatically.
Gap analysis — readiness before certification
Score current state against target. Identify the biggest gaps before your formal audit. Prioritise corrective actions. Walk into certification confident — not guessing.
